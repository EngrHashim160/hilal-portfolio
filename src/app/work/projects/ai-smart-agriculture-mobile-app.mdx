## Overview

This was my Final Year Project, built as an industrial collaboration with the Agriculture Research Institute Tarnab, Peshawar. The goal was to put practical AI in the hands of farmers and agricultural labs across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

The app combines machine learning, computer vision, and lab-tested soil data to deliver smart farming guidance tailored to local conditions.

## Key Features

- **Fertilizer Recommendations**: Data-driven fertilizer suggestions based on lab-tested soil samples.
- **Disease Detection**: Computer-vision models identify crop diseases from images.
- **Crop Yield Prediction**: Machine learning models forecast expected yield.
- **Nutrient Absorption Insights**: Guidance on how soil nutrients are absorbed by crops.

## Tech Stack

- Machine Learning & Computer Vision for predictions and disease detection
- Flutter for the cross-platform mobile application
- REST APIs connecting the mobile client to ML services
- Lab-tested soil datasets from the Agriculture Research Institute

## Outcome

The project demonstrates end-to-end applied AI: from raw soil and image data, through trained ML and CV models, to a usable mobile product that supports real agricultural decision-making for local farmers.
