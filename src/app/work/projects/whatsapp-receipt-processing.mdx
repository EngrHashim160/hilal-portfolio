## Overview

A WhatsApp-driven receipt processing automation built in n8n. Users send a receipt photo over WhatsApp, and the system extracts the details, files the image, and logs a structured record — all hands-free.

## How it works

- **Webhook trigger**: Incoming WhatsApp messages arrive via the GreenAPI webhook.
- **File handling**: The receipt image is downloaded and conditionally routed.
- **AI extraction**: An AI agent (OpenRouter chat model) extracts structured fields from the receipt.
- **Storage**: Files are uploaded to Google Drive and a row is appended to Google Sheets.
- **Reply**: Confirmation and results are sent back through the WhatsApp API.

## Tech Stack

n8n, GreenAPI (WhatsApp), OpenRouter LLM, Google Drive, Google Sheets, webhooks.

## Outcome

A conversational, zero-friction way to digitize receipts — turning a quick WhatsApp photo into a structured, stored, and logged record without any app or manual data entry.
