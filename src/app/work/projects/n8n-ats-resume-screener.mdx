## Overview

An automated Applicant Tracking System (ATS) built in n8n that screens incoming resumes without manual review, scoring each candidate against a structured rubric.

## How it works

- **Fetches** candidate emails and attachments from Outlook.
- **Parses** resumes from PDF and DOCX into structured text.
- **Matches** the job title against roles stored in Airtable.
- **Scores** each candidate with a strict 100-point ATS rubric.
- **Filters** by location and score threshold.
- **Shortlists** qualifying applicants to Google Drive and Airtable.

## Tech Stack

n8n, Microsoft Outlook, document parsing, AI scoring (LLM), Airtable, Google Drive.

## Outcome

A reliable, repeatable screening pipeline that turns a flood of inbound resumes into a ranked, filtered shortlist — saving hours of manual review while keeping scoring consistent.
