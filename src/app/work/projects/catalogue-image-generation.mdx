## Overview

A batch image-processing automation built in n8n that turns a spreadsheet of products into processed catalogue imagery using AI — with resilient handling for long-running predictions.

## How it works

- **Source**: Reads product rows from Google Sheets and downloads the original images.
- **AI routing**: An AI service router decides how each image is processed.
- **Replicate**: Starts a prediction on a Replicate model, then polls until completion.
- **Resilience**: Skips failed/throttled requests and waits for processing with retry loops.
- **Publish**: Uploads processed images to Google Drive, makes them public, and updates the sheet.

## Tech Stack

n8n, Replicate AI, Google Sheets, Google Drive, webhooks, batch loops with retry handling.

## Outcome

A scalable pipeline that processes catalogue images in bulk, gracefully handling rate limits and long-running AI jobs — replacing tedious one-by-one editing with a fully automated, sheet-driven flow.
