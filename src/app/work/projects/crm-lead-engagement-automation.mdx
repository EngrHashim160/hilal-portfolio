## Overview

A CRM engagement automation built in n8n that turns a raw lead event into a personalized, channel-aware outreach message — fully automatically.

## How it works

- **Webhook trigger**: Fires on an incoming lead event from Close CRM.
- **Enrichment**: Pulls lead info, contact, opportunities, and communications from the CRM API.
- **AI response**: Merges the lead data and generates a tailored reply with an OpenAI model.
- **Channel routing**: Decides between Email and SMS and sends through the matching Close CRM endpoint.

## Tech Stack

n8n, Close CRM API, OpenAI, webhooks, conditional channel routing.

## Outcome

An end-to-end lead engagement flow that responds to new leads instantly with personalized, context-aware messages on the channel that fits — keeping the pipeline warm without manual follow-up.
