## Overview

Built a complete AI automation system for Prime Solution Media that handles the full outreach lifecycle — from lead generation through to multi-channel content distribution — with minimal manual intervention.

## Capabilities

- **Lead Generation & Qualification**: Automated sourcing and scoring of leads in real time.
- **Approval & Campaign Enrollment**: Human-in-the-loop approval before campaigns launch.
- **CRM Tracking**: Synced lead and deal data across the CRM stack.
- **AI Blog Creation & Publishing**: Generated SEO content and published it to WordPress.
- **Multi-Channel Social Posting**: Distributed content across LinkedIn, X, and more.
- **Reply Handling**: Automated handling of incoming campaign replies.

## Tech Stack

OpenClaw, n8n, Airtable, Lemlist, HubSpot, Apollo, Google Places, WordPress, OmniSocials, OpenRouter, LinkedIn, X.

## Outcome

A unified, real-time automation pipeline that replaces fragmented manual outreach with a single orchestrated system spanning lead workflows, campaign launches, content generation, and social distribution.
