## Overview

A marketing intelligence platform that unifies call data, ad performance, and campaign insights behind a single AI-orchestrated interface.

## What it does

- **Call analysis**: Analyzes call transcripts and updates matched Airtable records.
- **Ad reporting**: Reports Google Ads performance and generates campaign insights.
- **Strategy workflows**: Supports SEO and social media strategy workflows.
- **Query routing**: A main AI orchestrator routes natural-language user queries to the correct data source.

## Tech Stack

n8n, Airtable, CallRail, Lead Docket, Google Ads API, OpenRouter/GPT models, webhooks, AI agent orchestration.

## Designed to scale

The architecture is built to expand into Meta Ads, SEO automation, social content generation, campaign optimization, and unified marketing reporting from one interface.

## Outcome

A single orchestrated system that replaces scattered marketing tools with one natural-language interface for analysis, reporting, and strategy.
